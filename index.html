Ellora Caves

Ellora Caves are a rock-cut Hindu temple cave complex, with artwork dating from the period 600-1000 CE, located in the Aurangabad District of Maharashtra, India. There are over 100 caves at the site, all excavated from the basalt cliffs in the Charanandri Hills, 34 of which are open to public.

Sanchi

Sanchi is a Buddhist complex, famous for its Great Stupa, on a hilltop at Sanchi Town in Raisen District of the State of Madhya Pradesh, India. It is located, about 23 kilometers from Raisen town, district headquarter and 46 kilometres north-east of Bhopal capital of Madhya Pradesh.
